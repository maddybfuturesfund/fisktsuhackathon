Mind and Wellness

College is a time when the physical and mental health of college students deteriorate due to multiple factors, stress being the most common. A survey of 96,000 college students across the United States reported that 44% of students had symptoms of depression, 37% had anxiety disorders and 15% heavily considered suicide(Heinze). In addition, countless amounts have shown that physical activity positively impacts mental health yet less than half of college students workout regularly.
